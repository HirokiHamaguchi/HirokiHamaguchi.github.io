Gallery

I went to TOKYO STATION GALLERY to see the exhibition "FOLON: AGENCY OF IMAGINARY JOURNEYS".

It was really fascinating. In particular, the screen printing was truly beautiful. I'm not sure if I have seen this kind of work before, but I was really impressed by its vivid coloring. Perhaps the aluminum canvas was essential for achieving such good coloring.
